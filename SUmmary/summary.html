My final project is a personal academic portfolio aimed at recruiters and family who want to learn more about who I am and what I have accomplished at USC. The site has three pages Home, Interests, and Experience. The Home page introduces me as an Electrical Engineering student at USC, highlights my background, and links to the rest of the site. The Interests page covers four things I am passionate about outside of school like beatboxing, Kirby, video games, and Mexican food where I used an alternating image and text layout. The Experience page shows my engineering projects like the HEYwire smart alarm clock, an electronic accordion built with a Qualcomm sponsored Rubik Pi, a temperature monitoring system built in EE109, and CMOS circuit research done under PhD mentorship. Features implemented include an HTML5 video tag for the HEYwire project, a consistent navbar and footer across all pages, and a centered column layout for the Experience page. A jQuery sticky navbar plugin will be added at a later date.
